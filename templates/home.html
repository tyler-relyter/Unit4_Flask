About Helldivers 2

Helldivers 2 is a third-person shooter game developed by Arrowhead Game Studios. It features cooperative gameplay and intense battles against alien forces.

Features

  • Cooperative multiplayer
  • Procedurally generated missions
  • Various weapons and equipment
